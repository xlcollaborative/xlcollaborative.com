Cross-License Collaborative Wishlist

Here are a few great ways to contribute:

  • Create a cross-license collaborative for your project and reach out by e-mail.

  • Read the latest terms and send feedback on GitHub or by e-mail.

  • Send questions by e-mail. The project is compiling an FAQ.

  • Make a logo for xlcollaborative.com. One idea: "castles in the air".
